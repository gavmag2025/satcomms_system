📖 CDR Export Workflow
1
Log in to ibisglobalbeam.satcomhost.com
2
Navigate to Installed Base → SIM Cards. Filter by ICCID to locate the SIM. Note the prepay balance shown in the row.
3
Click the ICCID link → jumps to Rated CDRs for that SIM (RatedCdrs.aspx?FC=ICCID&FV=...)
4
Use the Billing Period dropdown to select the month (format: YYYYMM, e.g. 202601)
5
CDR table shows: Service, Origin/Destination, Start/End time, Duration (minutes), CDR Price, Currency
6
Click Table Options → Download → choose Excel or PDF to export
